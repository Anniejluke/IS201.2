My Favorite Places

  1. Redfish Lake, ID

    • Beautiful views everywhere you look
    • You can rent paddleboards, boats, and other water toys to enjoy a nice day on the lake
    • Spotty service brings an opportunity to disconnect from the world
    • It's a place of many special and sentimental family memories and bonding time for me






  2. Provo Temple

    • Grass area behind the temple is a perfect spot for picnics, studies, and personal time
    • A great place to once again disconnect from the world, and connect with Heaven
    • Beautiful views to appreciate nature
    • I grew up having picnics at the temple every Sunday with my family, so it has become a special place for me!







  3. Provo Canyon

    • Beautiful views all year round
    • Plenty of activities such as skiing/snowboarding in the winter, outdoor concerts, tubing the river, hammoking in the trees, and ziplining







  4. Southern Florida

    • Gorgeous turquoise waters
    • Perfect spots for beach reading
    • White sand beaches
    • Warm year round!
    • Perfect family vacation spot!







  5. New York City

    • Incredible broadway shows every night of the week
    • Amazing food on every street
    • Beautiful city views
    • Great shopping
